Attend

 

  What is this about?

 

  • The AR/VR Festival is Boston University’s first and largest AR/VR exposition event.
  • Our event aims to connect heads of the industry, startups, scholars, and students at BU’s newest innovation hub, the BUild Lab.
  • There will be an assortment of promising students who have created projects and will be displaying them at individual tables in the hall, while each company will get their own room where they can showcase to attendees, as well as receiving stage time to do presentations.
